About Us

iAscend is a student-led nonprofit organization, funded by the University of Toronto, with the principal objective of equipping and empowering students with the necessary resources that they need to thrive within their respective institutions.

Our three primary objectives are:

(i) Leveling the field of opportunity to all students (ii) Providing students with the necessary resources to succeed in post-secondary (iii) Fostering creativity, leadership, and individuality
